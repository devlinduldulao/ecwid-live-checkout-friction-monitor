Help & Info

Understanding Your Data

Where is my real data?

Open this app from your Ecwid admin dashboard. The audit reads your real store profile, products, categories, and coupons automatically. No setup required.

What does the audit score mean?

The score (0–100) grades your store's checkout readiness based on catalog health, imagery, descriptions, inventory, and merchant contact info. Higher is better.

How do I know if data is real or sample?

Check the colored banner above the metrics. Green = live store data. Blue = sample/demo data. The Ecwid Sources panel below also shows each API call's status.

Settings are browser-local only

Your preferences and notes are stored in this browser. They won't appear on other devices or browsers. No data leaves your machine.

Static-Only Limits

What This Build Does Not Do

  • No live shopper event capture or visitor-side script injection.
  • No webhook ingestion, database, Redis, or always-on Node.js server.
  • No cross-device sync for notes or local dashboard preferences.
